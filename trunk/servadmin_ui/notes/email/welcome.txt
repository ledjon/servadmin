Welcome to the Email control panel!<br>
<br>
From here, you'll be able to do do the following functions:
<ul>
<li><b>Add / Delete Email Accounts</b> - Add new email accounts, or modify existing accounts.</li>
<li><b>Default Address</b> - Change the address that default (non-defined address) delivers to.</li>
<li><b>Email Forwards</b> - Create alias accounts that will forward to another email address.</li>
</ul>
If you have any additional needs, please contact our support staff.
